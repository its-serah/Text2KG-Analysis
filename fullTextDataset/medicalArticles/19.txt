LECITHIN thin-layer-chromatographic examinations in the maternal rat liver showed different rise of the triglycerides and a significant increase of the esterified cholesterol, however, no striking changes in the content of free cholesterol and lipid phosphorus with the fractions examined (lecithin, colamine cephalin, sphingomyelin and lysolecithin).
FRACTIONS thin-layer-chromatographic examinations in the maternal rat liver showed different rise of the triglycerides and a significant increase of the esterified cholesterol, however, no striking changes in the content of free cholesterol and lipid phosphorus with the fractions examined.
PLACENTA CORRESPOND phospholipid- and triglyceride concentrations of the placenta correspond to those of the fetal liver.
