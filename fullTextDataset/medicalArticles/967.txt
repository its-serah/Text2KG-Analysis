the effect of folic acid on growth and deoxyribonucleic acid synthesisin the rat kidney.   the effect of a single injection of folic acid (250mg/kg of body wt) on growth of the rat kidney was studied.the patterns of the response were found to be essentiallysimilar in animals aged between 3 wk and 12 mth.maximum values of kidney weight and nucleic acid content,relative to body weight, were found at 4 days atfolate administration.  the increases in wet and dryweights at this time were approx. 80% and 30% resp.
