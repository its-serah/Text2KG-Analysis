PLACENTAS total lipid and arachidonic acid were elevated in the placentas of preeclamptic women.
HYPERTENSION the amount and fatty acid composition of total lipid extract from serum were examined in normal pregnant women in the first, second, and third trimesters, and early postpartum period, and in patients with mild and severe preeclampsia and with essential hypertension.
FINDINGS the findings are compared with those obtained from animals developing eclampsia on an experimental basis.
ARACHIDONIC ACID total lipid and arachidonic acid were elevated in the placentas of preeclamptic women.
SERUM PALMITATE serum palmitate was mildly but significantly elevated in preeclampsia.
