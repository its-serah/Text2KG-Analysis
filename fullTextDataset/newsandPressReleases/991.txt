The new display includes objects, photos, and videos of linotype and monotype machines focusing on the way the machines expanded Government printing and played a role in the growth of the Government and the Nation during the 20th century. GPO has on display a linotype machine that was previously installed in a truck, creating a mobile print shop to produce essential war materials during World War I by General Pershing.
