This certification reinforces govinfo as a trustworthy source that performs digital preservation practices to ensure long-term preservation of and access to official Federal Government information. During the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic, GPO increased the amount of content it made available on govinfo all while making it easier for citizens to find and download Government information.
