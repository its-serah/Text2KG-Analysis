In his response, Director Halpern writes that he broadly intends to adopt the Task Force’s recommendations to move to a digital Federal Depository Library Program (FDLP) and has accounted for those few areas where the Agency may diverge from the recommendation as written. The Task Force on a Digital Federal Depository Library Program (Task Force), which was appointed by Director Halpern in early 2022, released its report recommending a digital FDLP in December 2022.
