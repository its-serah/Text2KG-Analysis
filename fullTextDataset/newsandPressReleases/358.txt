As a member of the Reaching Across Illinois Library System (RAILS), the Dixon Public Library allows patrons to visit any library in Illinois with their Dixon Public Library card. Through the FDLP, GPO partners with approximately 1,150 libraries nationwide to provide public access to authentic, published information from all three branches of the Federal Government in both digital and print formats.
