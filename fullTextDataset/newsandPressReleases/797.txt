WASHINGTON – The U.S. Government Printing Office (GPO) has issued a Request for Information (RFI) on options for commercial development of GPO-owned parking space behind the agency’s headquarters. Located within the North of Massachusetts Avenue (NOMA) Business Improvement District, the property is on H Street NW, across from one of the District’s new Walmart stores.
