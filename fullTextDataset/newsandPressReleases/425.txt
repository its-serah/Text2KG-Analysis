The Library periodically invites the members of Hawaiʻi’s congressional delegation to visit the Government Documents Collection. The Library has been a regional depository since 1977. In addition to the State of Hawaiʻi, the Library also serves American Samoa, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, Guam, and the Federated States of Micronesia.
