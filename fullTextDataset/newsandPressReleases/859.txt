Our existing aluminium double-glazed windows are now over 40 years old; they have outlived their life expectancy and it is time to replace them. The replacement of the windows will improve the environmental conditions of the repositories contributing to the long-term preservation of our collection, as well as being more energy efficient (in keeping with our sustainability plans) and therefore potentially saving money.
