Washington – The U.S. Government Publishing Office (GPO) achieves the global standard of excellence for the agency’s production of the U.S. passport and secure Federal credentials. GPO’s production facilities in Washington, D.C. have achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification. GPO attained ISO certification in 2010 and this recertification has been achieved by meeting the new standards and demonstrating a long standing practice of consistency. ISO is the International Organization for Standardization and the world’s largest developer of international standards used by Government, business and new information technology companies and agencies.
