AAS president, Ellen S. Dunlap. WASHINGTON – The U.S. Government Printing Office (GPO) continues its commitment to providing employees a discrimination-free work environment. There were 17 formal equal employment opportunity (EEO) complaints filed in FY 2014, a 66% decrease from the previous year. The complaints represent the fewest number filed in the last decade. Diversity in the workplace and minimizing and resolving EEO complaints is an ongoing commitment of GPO’s Strategic Plan.
