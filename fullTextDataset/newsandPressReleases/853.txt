Harry Truman and the two Marx Brothers had gone through their lives together, distantly aware of one another for at least some important part of their lives. There is a mutual warmth and admiration evident in the correspondence between Truman and Groucho and Harpo Marx that goes beyond what one would expect from correspondents so slightly acquainted.
