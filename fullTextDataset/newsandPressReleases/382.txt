This past year also marked the 10th anniversary of GPO’s production of secure identification cards for Federal agencies. Since 2008, GPO has served as an integrator of secure identification smart cards to support the credentialing requirements of Federal agencies and other Government entities. To date, GPO has produced more than 21 million secure credential cards across 11 different product lines.
