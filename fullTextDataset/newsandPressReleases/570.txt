GPO is currently in the process of determining which contractors will continue performance, in the event of a Government shutdown. If the decision is made to shutdown, Acquisition Services will issue notification letters/emails to the contractors that GPO has identified as needing to continue performance. For all contracts that have been fully funded, performance is to continue uninterrupted. All other contractors (contracts not fully funded) will need to discontinue performance.
