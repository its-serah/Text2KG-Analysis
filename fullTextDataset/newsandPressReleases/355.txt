WASHINGTON — The U.S. Government Publishing Office (GPO) welcomes Dixon Public Library in Dixon, IL to the Federal Depository Library Program (FDLP). Illinois Senator Tammy Duckworth designated the library as a Federal depository. As a member of the FDLP, the library will provide its residents free access to Federal Government information in both digital and print formats.
