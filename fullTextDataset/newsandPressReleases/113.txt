For almost 60 years Graphic Design USA (GDUSA) has been a business-to-business information source for graphic design professionals. GDUSA sponsors its American Graphic Design Awards, a competition that spotlights areas of excellence and opportunity for creative professionals. Over 10,000 entries were submitted and a highly selective 10% were chosen. Winning designs are eligible for publishing in the 2023 GDUSA Design Annual later this year.
