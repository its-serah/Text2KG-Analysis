Louisiana State University’s Middleton Library and Louisiana Tech University’s Prescott Memorial Library, and their predecessor libraries, have been providing services to the residents of Louisiana since the 19th century. These regional FDLP libraries ensure Government documents are accessible and up-to-date for their patrons. They helped work with Southern University in New Orleans to reopen its library almost a decade after the destruction left behind by Hurricane Katrina.
