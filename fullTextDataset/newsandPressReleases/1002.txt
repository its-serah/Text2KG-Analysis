PA. The United States is celebrating the 225th anniversary of the adoption of the Constitution of the United States. The U.S. Government Printing Office (GPO) makes the Constitution available to Congress and the public in a variety of formats. GPO produces the pocket version of the Constitution for Members of Congress to hand out to their constituents and also makes it available through GPO’s retail and online bookstores.
