Susanne Caro: Government Information Librarian, North Dakota State University Libraries. Caro is the Government Information Librarian at North Dakota State University where she assists users in accessing Government information. She is also a joint regional coordinator of the Federal Depository Library Program for North Dakota. She was the editor and an author of Digitizing Your Collection (ALA, 2016), and Government Information Essentials (ALA, 2018), which won the 2019 Margaret T. Lane/Virginia F.
