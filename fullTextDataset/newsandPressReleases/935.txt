WASHINGTON – As the U.S. Supreme Court makes it decision about the Voting Rights Act of 1965, the U.S. Government Printing Office (GPO) makes the authentic, digital version of the law available on the agency’s Federal Digital System (FDsys). GPO authenticated the document by digital signature. This signature assures the public that the document has not been changed or altered.
