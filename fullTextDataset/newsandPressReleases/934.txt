GPO operates in partnership with FedEx Office, has achieved a milestone of 100,000 orders since it began operation in 2005. GPOExpress provides Federal agencies with discounted services to meet their small and short turnaround printing and binding needs. By enrolling in the program, Federal agency employees receive a GPOExpress card that allows them to take advantage of pre-negotiated prices that are up to 80 percent below retail rates.
