This year marks the 50th anniversary of the Depository Library Act of 1962, landmark legislation that expanded the scope of Federal publications distributed to depository libraries and created the current system of regional and selective depository libraries nationwide. The act resulted in a vast increase in the libraries participating in the FDLP and more publications being distributed to each library, thus providing the public with greater access to Government information.
