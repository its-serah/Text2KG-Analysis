WASHINGTON—The U.S. Government Publishing Office (GPO) has achieved the goal of reducing its workforce by 5% after offering employee buyouts during the first quarter of FY 2015. A total of 103 employees took GPO’s buyout. GPO’s total workforce today is at 1,699 employees, the fewest in the past century. Since 1980, GPO has reduced its workforce by 74% as the result of using new technologies, a rate of change unparalleled elsewhere
