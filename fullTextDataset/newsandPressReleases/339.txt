The Max Chambers Library was designated a depository by Senator Will Rogers in July 1935, and over the ensuing decades has provided Government information to faculty, staff, students, and the community. The library was an early partner in the FDLP's transition to digital formats and tools, hosting the “Browse Topics” service for several years.
