GPO has been making the Budget available online since the 1990s and this is the second year that GPO developed a mobile web app for the Federal Budget. GPO produces the Federal Budget for the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) and production of this important document can be traced back to the Budget and Accounting Act of 1921.
