GPO. The Worcester, MA institution has been providing free, public access to our country’s official documents since authorized by Congress to do so on December 1, 1814. It was on that day, when President James Madison signed the 7th Joint Congressional Resolution of the 3rd session of the 13th Congress, authorizing the Federal Government “for furnishing the American Antiquarian Society with a copy of the Journals of Congress and of the documents published under their order."
