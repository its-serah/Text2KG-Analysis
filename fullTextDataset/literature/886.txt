Ah! you are indignant at the arrogance of the old aristocracy; well, on your knees, by Jupiter! on your face, rather, before the golden crown on field of gules.” No one replied: every one was trying his best to hear. In the parlor, between M. Favoral and M. de Thaller, a discussion of the utmost violence was evidently going on.
