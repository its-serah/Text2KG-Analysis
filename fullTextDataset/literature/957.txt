The sun streamed in through his windows and lighted a room finished in wainscoting of oak and maple--sawed at the mill, which was part of the institution and which he could see from his window, when he looked down. Above, when he cast his eyes in that direction through another window, nestled the small hospital, where barbaric methods of local surgery were being altered.
