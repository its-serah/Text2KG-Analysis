"I didn't mean to be." Her eyes rounded. She was puzzled. She was thinking. I could see, even on that short acquaintance, that thinking was always going to be a bother to her. "Handsome too," she said. "And I bet you know it." I grunted. "What's your name?" "Reilly," I said. "Doghouse Reilly." "That's a funny name."
