He would "jest natcherly take his foot in his hand and light out"--pausing only for a little "snack" to eat and a flask to cheer his journey. He made his way slowly down into the center of the town: a town which had come to recognize at a glance these prison-given suits of black; these faces pasty with the pallor of confinement; this shamble fathered by the slouchy swing of the lock-step.
