Deacon. Old McAllister Falkins rose with a suppressed exclamation of dismay, and Henry bit off an oath. Black Pete Spooner rode along at an easy amble, and outside the fence he drew rein and sang out in a grave and utterly unembarrassed voice: "Gentlemen, may I alight and have speech with you?" The two Falkinses rose and walked down to meet the unexpected visitor, uncertain what attitude to take in the face of such stupendous effrontery.
