"Was yer--was yer in de pen'tenshery, boss?" The man chained to his rock pile doubted the wisdom of his question, but African inquisitiveness had mastered his better judgment. Instantly he recognized his mistake. The boy from Troublesome was at once on his feet and his sallow face was distorted with anger. From his lips came profane volleys of abuse.
