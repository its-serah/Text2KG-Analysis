Take ten deep breaths, as slowly as possible. (You may prefer to do this with your eyes closed.)Now focus on the rise and fall of your rib cage, and the air moving in and out of your lungs. Noticethe sensations as the air flows in: your chest rising, your shoulders lifting, your lungs expanding.Notice what you feel as the air flows out: your chest falling, your shoulders dropping, the breathleaving your nostrils. Focus on completely emptying your lungs. Push out every last bit of air,feeling your lungs deflate, and pause
